ITZY is set to share “Boys Like You,” the new pre-release English single.


The group continues to accelerate its rise in global popularity by sharing its new English EP, “Boys Like You,” at 1 p.m. (midnight EST) on October 21.“Boys Like You” is a track full of the group’s signature bold and daring energy. Its frank and straightforward lyrics send a warning to the person who let the singers down, emphasizing the point that they “won’t ever fall for boys like you again.” With its pop sound made even fuller with a touch of funk, the track boasts an intensely catchy melody as its hook. Didrik Thott and Sebastian Thott, the Swedish composers behind “Sneakers,” the quintet’s hit track that took over the K-pop scene this summer, participated in the song’s production.
