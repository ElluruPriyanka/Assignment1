ABOUT US


ITZY (있지; debuted Feb 11, 2019) is a South Korean girl group under JYP Entertainment (JYPE). They debuted on February 11, 2019. The five members of ITZY are: Lia, Yeji, Yuna, Chaeryeong, and Ryujin.
