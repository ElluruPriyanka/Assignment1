On November 30th, ITZY releases a new Mini Album, “CHESHIRE,” and the title track, “Cheshire.”


ITZY reached their highest ranks yet, with their 5th EP, “CHECKMATE,” and the title track, “SNEAKERS.” They reached 8th Place on “Billboard 200,” the US Billboard chart, and also 10th Place on “Artist 100,” where artists are ranked through a comprehensive analysis of their results. The album received 720,665 pre-orders and added another 472,394 initial Chodong (first-week) orders based on the real-time music chart website Hanteo Charts. On August 31st, the album surpassed the 1 million threshold on Circle Chart figures, reaching the million-seller list and proving the quintet’s clear “career-high.”
