> CRAZY IN LOVE


- 3rd collaboration with hit-making music production team GALACTIKA *, to follow the success of “DALLA DALLA” and “WANNABE”


- Title track “LOCO,” ITZY’s ‘crazy into you’ kind of love song! Spoken like a true gen Z, the lyrics captures that honest and instinctive gravitational pull towards love.


- The FIRST full-length album we’ve all been desperately waiting for! ITZY eyes K-pop’s 2021 second half with a new lead single.


ITZY will release their very first full-length album 〈CRAZY IN LOVE〉 on September 24th. A thrilling, highly-anticipated comeback with title track “LOCO” is scheduled to get a simultaneous worldwide release


ITZY’s previous EP 〈GUESS WHO〉 with lead single “Mafia in the morning” saw much success on the charts since its unveiling on April 30th this year, landing on Billboard’s competitive main charts “Billboard 200” and “Artist 100” while outdoing the group’s previous record on global music platform Spotify’s “Global Top 200.” Established now as a powerful performance-centric girl group, ITZY makes another big step towards the global stage with their first full-length album since debut 〈CRAZY IN LOVE〉.

‘마.피.아. In the morning’ catches the audience’s eyes with the unique song name and extraordinary visual concepts. The supreme performance of the members on top of the addictive sounds are gaining explosive popularity, coining a new expression, getting mafia-ed(from the song ‘Mafia In The Morning’). The song was produced by the producer who represents JYP, Jinyoung Park, and also was collaborated with leading composers like LYRE, earattack, KASS, Haesol Lee(이해솔), and Danke. The new single’s lyrics are written with Sophiya.
